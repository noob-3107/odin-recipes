Maggi

Description

Maggi noodles are dried noodles fused with oil, and sold with a packet of flavorings. These noodles are usually eaten after being cooked in boiling water.

Steps are simple:
  1. Cut the packet.
  2. Take out Maggi
  3. Take a bowl of water and make it boil
  4. Add maggi
  5. Cook till only a bit raw
  6. Add maggi masala
  7. Maggi is ready
